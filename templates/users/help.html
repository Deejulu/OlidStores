Getting Started

Create Your Account
  1. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
  2. Fill in your email, username, and password
  3. Verify your email (if required)
  4. Complete your profile with delivery details
Browse Products
  1. Visit the Shop page
  2. Use filters to narrow down by category, price
  3. Search for specific products using the search bar
  4. Click on any product to view full details

Complete Shopping Guide

Methods to find products:

  • Browse by Category: Navigate through Electronics, Cosmetics, Fashion, Home & more
  • Use Search Bar: Type product names or keywords at the top of any page
  • Filter Results: Sort by price, popularity, newest arrivals
  • View Featured: Check homepage for special deals and featured items
Tip: Use the wishlist to save products you're interested in for later!

  2. Review product images, description, and specifications

  4. Choose variants like size, color (if applicable)

  6. Continue shopping or go to cart to checkout

Cart Features:

  • View cart icon in top navigation showing item count
  • Update quantities directly in cart
  • Remove items you no longer want
  • See live total calculation including delivery fees

Checkout Steps:

  1. Click "Proceed to Checkout" from your cart
  2. Review your order summary and delivery details
  3. Enter or confirm your delivery address
  4. Add phone number and any special delivery instructions
  5. Choose your preferred payment method
  6. Review total amount including delivery fee
  7. Complete payment or upload receipt
Note: Ensure all delivery information is accurate before completing your order. Orders cannot be modified after submission.

Payment Methods

Paystack (Instant Payment)

Advantages:

  • Instant order confirmation
  • Secure payment gateway
  • Multiple payment options (Card, Bank Transfer, USSD)
  • Immediate order processing

How it works:

  1. Select Paystack at checkout
  2. You'll be redirected to secure payment page
  3. Choose payment method (Card/Bank/USSD)
  4. Complete payment
  5. Automatic order confirmation
Recommended
Manual Bank Transfer

Process:

  1. Select Manual Bank Transfer at checkout
  2. View our bank account details
  3. Make transfer from your bank
  4. Upload payment receipt/screenshot
  5. Wait for admin confirmation (within 24 hours)
  6. Order will be processed after verification
Processing Time: Manual payments are verified within 24 hours. Your order will be on hold until payment is confirmed by our team.

Order Tracking & Status

Track your orders from your Dashboard or Order History page.

Order Status Explained:
Status Meaning What's Next
Pending Order received, awaiting payment confirmation Complete payment or wait for manual payment verification
Processing Payment confirmed, order being prepared Your order is being packed and prepared for shipment
Shipped Order dispatched for delivery Track your package using the tracking number provided
Delivered Order successfully delivered Enjoy your purchase! Don't forget to leave a review
Cancelled Order cancelled by you or admin Refund will be processed if payment was made
Notifications: You'll receive email notifications for each status update on your order.
